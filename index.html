 NRF-Korea SSK project 
AI and Polarization: Towards inclusive communication infrastructure

Our work aims to comprehensively diagnose political, social, economic, and cultural polarization generated by AI-driven platform technologies across both online and offline dimensions, and to propose technological, social, and institutional intervention strategies that enable AI technologies to function as social infrastructure for building an inclusive communication environment.
